The Science

Four pillars of human learning, built into every interaction

1

Bloom's Taxonomy Progression

Every conversation moves Beauty Advisors up through Remember, Understand, Apply, Analyse, Evaluate, and Create. Not stuck at surface recall.

2

Spaced Retrieval Practice

Beauty Byte challenges are timed to the Ebbinghaus forgetting curve. Knowledge is tested at the precise moment it begins to fade.

3

Multi-Perspective Characters

Eight teaching characters offer different cognitive perspectives. A Foundation Builder explains differently from a Challenge Coach or Pattern Connector.

4

Emotional Adaptation

Six emotional archetypes detected through conversation signals. Tone, pacing, and character selection adapt to how the learner is feeling.
