Why This Matters — The Science Behind the Experience

Every enhancement is grounded in peer-reviewed learning science and human psychology

Neuroscience

Spaced Retrieval Practice

Beauty Byte challenges are timed to each BA's forgetting curve (Ebbinghaus, 1885). Retrieving product knowledge at the optimal interval strengthens memory traces far more effectively than re-reading modules. These aren't quizzes — they're precision-timed memory reinforcement that builds real counter confidence.

Social Learning

Airport & Regional Peer Momentum

Bandura's Social Learning Theory shows we're wired to learn from observing others. Showing trending topics across airports and team activity creates healthy social momentum — not competition, but collective curiosity. When a BA in Singapore sees what's trending in Seoul or Tokyo, knowledge flows across the region.

xAPI Intelligence

Expertise Profiling & Workforce Intelligence

Every interaction generates xAPI statements — "Wei Ling demonstrated Skincare Science at Analyse level." These build a living competency profile per BA, visible to both the learner and the organisation. L'Oréal Travel Retail gains real-time visibility into where expertise sits across airports, which product areas have knowledge gaps, and where to direct training investment. This is workforce intelligence, not vanity metrics.

Emotional Adaptation

Meeting Every BA Where They Are

Zavmo detects emotional signals from natural conversation and adapts tone, character, and difficulty in real time. A new BA in their first week gets warmth and scaffolding; a seasoned advisor gets challenged with complex customer scenarios. Learning becomes as unique as a fingerprint.

Bloom's Taxonomy

From Recall to Consultative Selling

The standard chatbot stays at Remember level — retrieve and repeat. Zavmo's characters climb Bloom's taxonomy deliberately, moving BAs from understanding ingredients to analysing customer needs to creating personalised recommendations. The Challenge Coach doesn't give answers — it builds consultants.

Character Rotation

Eight Perspectives, Deeper Encoding

Eight distinct teaching characters present product knowledge from different angles — the Foundation Builder uses metaphor, the Evidence Evaluator demands proof, the Pattern Connector reveals links between skincare science and selling technique. Multi-perspective encoding creates richer neural pathways than a single chatbot voice.

Knowledge Base

eLearning Modules, Document Materials & More

The knowledge base indexes eLearning modules (title, description, URL), Document materials (title, description, URL, PDF contents), and scraped external links and FAQs. Zavmo's enhanced companion meets every requirement and extends it — answers cite their source module for trust and traceability, creating a single unified knowledge layer.

Starter Questions

Personalised, Not Generic

The spec requires floating starter questions. The standard approach offers the same generic prompts to every BA. Zavmo generates personalised starter questions based on what the learner studied recently, their knowledge gaps, and trending topics across their region — turning an FAQ shortcut into a curiosity springboard.
