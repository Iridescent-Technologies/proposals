How Zavmo Delivers

The 4D Learning Process

Every learner in each stream moves through Zavmo's four-phase cycle. The process begins with understanding the individual, designs a personalised pathway, delivers adaptive learning through conversation, and builds a verified evidence portfolio mapped to every Learning Outcome and Assessment Criterion.

D1 — Discover
Discover
Zavmo's Intelligent Needs Analysis profiles each learner across four dimensions: current competencies, target competencies, skills benchmarked against professional standards, and qualification pathways. AI-powered gap analysis then triangulates this data to create a personalised development plan.
1Analyse existing competencies (current role)
2Map target competencies (desired role)
3Benchmark against professional standards
4Match to qualification pathways
✓Neurodiversity & learning style profiling
D2 — Discuss
Discuss
A collaborative design conversation where the learner's individual pathway is shaped — aligning their goals, role context and preferences with the programme's Learning Outcomes and Assessment Criteria.
✓Personalised learning pathway designed
✓Goals aligned to programme LOs & ACs
✓Pace & sequence adapted to the individual
✓Role & institutional context integrated
D3 — Deliver
Deliver
Zavmo deploys 12 specialist AI learning companions — each with a distinct teaching approach. From Foundation Builder (scaffolding new knowledge) to Pattern Connector (visual mind-mapping for ADHD) and Systems Analyst (systematic precision for autism) — the right companion is matched to each learner's needs.
✓12 AI companions with distinct approaches
✓Neurodiversity-specific characters (ADHD, dyslexia, autism)
✓Cognitive load optimised (neuroscience-backed)
✓Progress tracked against each LO & AC
D4 — Demonstrate
Demonstrate
Zavmo builds a verified evidence portfolio that maps directly to each Assessment Criterion. Evidence is captured continuously through the learning journey — not bolted on at the end — proving genuine capability development.
✓Evidence mapped to every AC
✓Continuous portfolio build (not end-loaded)
✓Measurable skills uplift & ROI data
✓Assessment-ready output for each learner

The same 4D process, three different domains. Each stream receives identical Zavmo delivery — the differentiation is the programme content and domain context, not the method. This demonstrates Zavmo's versatility: one platform, three entirely different disciplines, all with personalised, evidence-based learning.

Pilot Timeline

Implementation Overview

The pilot runs across three phases: setup and discovery, live 4D delivery across all three streams, and outcomes reporting. Each phase maps directly to the 4D process.

Weeks 1–6 — Setup & Discovery

Three-Stream Discovery & Programme Design

Separate discovery sessions with each faculty lead. Zavmo conducts a Training Needs Analysis per stream, mapping UM6P's institutional priorities to each programme's Learning Outcomes and Assessment Criteria. Programme content configured for Sustainability, Technology Management and Entrepreneurship domains. Language and accessibility requirements established per stream.

4D Phase: Preparation for Discover
Week 7 — Cohort Onboarding

60 Learners Onboarded — Discover Phase Begins

All 60 learners onboarded across three stream environments. Each learner completes their individual Discover phase: conversational profiling, learning style and neurodiversity assessment, prior learning recognition, and baseline capability mapping against their programme's LOs. Individual learning pathways designed through the Discuss phase.

4D Phases: Discover & Discuss
Weeks 8–22 — Live Learning

Adaptive Delivery Across All Three Streams

All 60 learners actively progressing through their personalised pathways. Zavmo delivers adaptive conversational learning mapped to each programme's LOs and ACs. Evidence portfolios build continuously through the Demonstrate phase. Mid-point review at Week 14 with comparative data across all three streams. Progress tracked per learner, per unit, per AC.

4D Phases: Deliver & Demonstrate
Weeks 23–26 — Outcomes & Report

Three-Stream Outcomes Report & Rollout Proposal

Comprehensive outcomes report covering all three streams. Completion rates, evidence portfolio quality, learner satisfaction and measurable skills uplift per stream. Cross-stream analysis demonstrating Zavmo's versatility. ROI calculation versus UM6P's current delivery. Fully costed rollout proposal for university-wide adoption.

Outcome: Evidence-based rollout case

Why 60 Learners — Not 250
Zavmo's standard commercial model begins at 250 learners (Foundation tier). This pilot is deliberately scoped at 60 learners across three streams as a Strategic Innovation Partnership — offered at preferential terms for three reasons:
1 — Proof of Concept
Three streams across three faculties demonstrates Zavmo's versatility at a controlled scale — building the evidence base for a 250+ learner Phase 2 rollout with confidence.
2 — Joint Innovation Showcase
UM6P becomes a flagship innovation partner. Pilot outcomes will be jointly submitted for international education and AI awards — building on Zavmo's Training Industry Award win (2025).
3 — Published Case Study
A co-branded case study documenting outcomes across all three streams — positioning UM6P as a global leader in AI-driven learning and providing Zavmo with its first pan-African deployment reference.
Pilot-to-Scale pathway: Phase 1 (60 learners) validates the model across three disciplines. Phase 2 begins at 250 learners (Foundation tier) with the full white-label pricing structure — giving UM6P a clear, de-risked path from pilot to university-wide deployment.
