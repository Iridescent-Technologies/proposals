The Science

Powered by a Small Language Model Trained on Neuroscience

At the heart of Zavmo is something no other learning platform has: a proprietary Small Language Model (SLM) specifically trained on the neuroscience of how human brains form, strengthen, and retain knowledge. This is not a generic AI bolted onto a course library. This is a purpose-built intelligence that understands learning at a neurological level.

Neural Pathway Formation

Every time you learn something, your brain creates a new neural connection. Zavmo’s Mind Palace visualises these connections in real time, showing you exactly how new knowledge links to what you already know — reinforcing deeper understanding.

Spaced Repetition

Your brain forgets information at a predictable rate. Zavmo uses scientifically-timed review intervals to resurface concepts just before you would forget them, turning short-term memories into permanent knowledge with minimal effort.

Active Recall

Passively reading slides does almost nothing for retention. Zavmo’s AI characters engage you in dialogue, ask you questions, challenge your assumptions, and require you to articulate your understanding — the single most effective way to lock knowledge into long-term memory.

Growth Mindset Detection

Zavmo’s AI monitors your language patterns in real time. When it detects fixed-mindset thinking (“I can’t do this”), it gently reframes the conversation to build resilience and confidence — a technique proven to improve learning outcomes.

Neurodiversity by Design

Every brain works differently. Zavmo celebrates neurodiversity with adaptive AI characters designed for different cognitive styles — whether you learn best through hands-on practice, structured analysis, or creative exploration. No one is left behind.

Contextual Relevance

Your brain retains information far better when it connects to your real-world context. Zavmo maps every piece of content directly to your job role and career goals, so everything you learn is immediately applicable to your daily work.

The Invisible Genius

Agent 13: The Flow State Orchestrator

An invisible meta-agent that sits above all 12 teaching characters, continuously adjusting their behaviour to maximise learning impact — without the learner ever knowing.

Built on Csíkszentmihályi’s Flow Theory

The scientifically proven state where learning is most effective — the balance between challenge and ability.

Agent 13 monitors every learner interaction in real time, detecting when a learner is in the flow state, when they are becoming frustrated (challenge too high), or when they are becoming bored (challenge too low). It then dynamically adjusts how the 12 teaching characters behave — modifying difficulty, switching teaching approaches, or changing the character entirely — to keep each learner in their optimal learning zone.

Observer Mode

Silently monitors engagement signals, cognitive load, and emotional indicators across all interactions

Recommendation Mode

Suggests adjustments to teaching characters — modify tone, increase scaffolding, or shift cognitive level

Override Mode

Directly intervenes to switch characters, adjust difficulty, or trigger a different pedagogical approach entirely

For admins: Agent 13’s orchestration data provides unprecedented insight into workforce learning patterns. See which teams thrive under challenge, which need more scaffolding, and how your organisation’s collective learning culture is evolving over time — all powered by real-time xAPI analytics.
